The new EM&Q
mobile/ tablet application

A new application dedicated to teachers and academic representatives, to educate themselves, their peers and students, about gender equality. In a fun, educative way.

The Issue

Educators may not provide equal opportunities for boys and girls. This reinforces the existing gender bias that most often disadvantages girls. Training standards and the quality of ECCE training in some countries do not adequately prepare ECCE educators to be gender sensitive. Administrators and supervisors do not see the importance of gender-sensitive education or have the knowledge and competency in promoting gender equality to support educators.

The Solution

How can a digital tool inspire and influence academic representative to implement gender equality in their schooling?

Our application will allow professors and academic individuals to retrieve course materials or documentation when preparing for the course content about subjects related to gender equality. It also can be used as check list kind of board with different activities to be applied in school during a given lesson related to gender equality.

The app will provide different educational materials to gain awareness of gender equality issues present in the society and how to fight against it through pedagogical methods and practices.

This app will help educate a young generation to have a better understanding of the concept of gender equality and erase the gender biases found in society nowadays.

The Application

Targeted audience: Considering that early childhood years are critical for cognitive, social and emotional development of boys and girls, the project involves and engages educational institutions representatives, precisely professors and academic individuals because they detain the key role to take social and gender discrimination out of early childhood education and replace it with empowerment.

Project EM&Q is a mobile app linked to a database storing a collection of useful documentation and school resources to be used by professors and academic individuals to implement it in their courses in order to promote gender equality and make education more gender-sensitive.

The course content will contain a variety of documents, reports, and research revolving around the topic of gender equality, and also best practices for the teachers to get inspired from. Moreover, the application provides a more detailed explanation of each course, and also a list of the trending courses and top-rated ones for the teachers to always stay in “the now” in terms of gender equality subjects.

Our main focus is to offer a comprehensive content about “gender equality” for teacher to access easily and make use of it in their courses. Also, for those who would like to keep track of the students’ progress, the application provides the possibility to input all the students results and access them when needed.

The Features

1.

Access/download pedagogical content/material

2.

Suggest similar content to the ones researched

3.

Suggest “Most trending” or “Top-rated” content

4.

Store students' results (following exercises, assignments, or activities)

5.

Provide an overview of the students' grades

6.

Bookmark a given content to access it later
